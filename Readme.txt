Project Name: Bands

Purpose: The purpose of Bands is to increase my learning abilities to build an iOS Application. Bands is used to keep track of all the bands you never heard of out in public and you would later like to go back and learn more about them on your free will.

App Features:

Add any number of bands.
Take notes about a band.
Take pictures of a band.
Tell your friends about a band.
Search the web for a band.
Find places to buy merchandise from a band.
Preview tracks of a band.
